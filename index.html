Frequently Asked Questions

Is it free to participate?

Yes, completely free. No fees, no subscriptions, no catch. You build, we ship.

What counts as a valid project?

Anything you genuinely built a game, a website, a hardware project, an API. It just needs to be functional and submitted before the program deadline.

How long does shipping take after approval?

Every YSWS program has a different reward and shipping timeline, which is outlined in the "How to Participate" section of each program. Some rewards are digital and delivered, while physical rewards typically ship after project approval.

Can I join more than one program at a time?

Yes! Just make sure to submit a project for each program you're participating in before their respective deadlines. Each program is independent, so you can participate in as many as you'd like!

What happens if my project gets rejected?

You'll receive feedback on why it wasn't approved. Depending on the reason, you may be able to make adjustments and resubmit before the deadline
